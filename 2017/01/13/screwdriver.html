Screwdriver.cd released with bilingual documentation by Locki

January 13, 2017

Yahoo’s open-source project Screwdriver, a Continuous Delivery Build System for Dynamic Infrastructure, was just released with all product docs available in English and Japanese.

This community-driven tool will simplify deployment pipelines, optimize for trunk development, and make rolling back easier. Screwdriver handles over 25,000+ builds per day and 12,000+ daily git commits as a single shared entrypoint for Yahoo. It supports multiple languages and handles both virtual machine and container-based builds and deployment.

While Yahoo has been filling the headlines with drastic strategic decisions lately, we are very proud to support Yahoo’s open-source project and take the collaborative results to new places in the world.

Thanks to a supportive community and its collaborative translation integrated with Locki, Screwdriver docs are now available to developers in 2 languages.

We wish Screwdriver.cd continuous success, with continuous and additional language support!

More information about the Screwdriver project can be found on the official website, within their newest blog post, or under the SD Times news.

Do not forget to chek out the bilingual docs in English and Japanese and integrate Locki for your (open-source) projects.
